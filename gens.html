Discription

With a high-resolution 108 MP triple back camera and a Sony-powered 32 MP front camera, the Gen z1 5G unleashes the photographer in you. The phone's expressive beauty is brought out by the LDI processing technology, making it a cherished asset. You can easily shoot beautiful photographs with this phone's Smart Skin Tone Protection AI. This phone is quite light, weighing roughly 165 g and having a thickness of 7.41 mm, making it very handy for you. You can multitask with ease thanks to an outstanding 12 GB of RAM that converts unused storage into RAM and allows for up to 8 GB of RAM extension. You can have a flawless 5G experience thanks to the 6 nm Octa-core 5G CPU. With multi-layer graphite sheets, a iquid cooling system, and Borazon insulation, you won't have to worry about your phone overheating during a long gaming session.
